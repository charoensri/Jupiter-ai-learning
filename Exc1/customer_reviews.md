**Common Product Issues**

- **Poor build quality / early failure**  
  *Many customers report the item breaking after only a few days or weeks, or functioning correctly for a very short time before becoming unusable.*  
  **Frequency:** *mentioned frequently*

- **Misleading product description or photos**  
  *Several reviews say the item looks nothing like the advertised pictures, or that the features described online are absent or over‑promised.*  
  **Frequency:** *mentioned frequently*

- **Cheap or low‑grade materials**  
  *A number of reviewers note that the materials feel flimsy, that the finish looks cheap, or that the product feels “broken on first use.”*  
  **Frequency:** *mentioned frequently*

- **Performance not as advertised**  
  *Many comments describe the product not delivering the promised results—e.g., not working as expected, limited functionality, or insufficient power/efficiency.*  
  **Frequency:** *mentioned frequently*

- **Missing or insufficient features / design limitations**  
  *Some customers point out that the product lacks expected features, has a small learning curve, or has minor design flaws that hinder usability.*  
  **Frequency:** *occasional complaint*

- **Unclear or incomplete instructions**  
  *A few reviewers mention that the instruction manual is hard to follow or missing key steps.*  
  **Frequency:** *occasional complaint*

- **Limited colour/variant options**  
  *One review notes the lack of colour choices as a drawback.*  
  **Frequency:** *rare issue*

**Common Service Issues**

- **Delayed or late shipping**  
  *At least one review specifically states that delivery was slower than promised.*  
  **Frequency:** *occasional complaint*

- **Damaged product on arrival**  
  *One reviewer reported the item arriving damaged.*  
  **Frequency:** *rare issue*

- **Unhelpful or unresponsive customer support**  
  *A couple of comments complain that customer service did not resolve their problems or was unhelpful.*  
  **Frequency:** *occasional complaint*

- **Refund or return refusal**  
  *One negative review indicates that the seller refused to refund after a damaged or unsatisfactory item was sent back.*  
  **Frequency:** *rare issue*

- **Fast shipping (positive)**  
  *A few reviews highlight that shipping was quick, which is a positive service note but not a complaint.*  
  **Frequency:** *occasional positive note*
